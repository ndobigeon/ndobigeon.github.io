  • Méthodes de synthèse

    • T. W. Parks and J. McClellan, "Chebyshev approximation for non recursive digital filters with linear phase," IEEE. Trans. Circuit Theory, vol. 19, no. 2, pp. 189-194, March 1972.
    • N. Ahmed and K. R. Rao, "Recursive techniques for obtaining Padé approximants," Proc. of the IEEE, vol. 56, no. 6, pp. 1101-1102, June 1968.
    • F. Brophy and A. Salazar, "Considerations of the Padé approximant technique in the synthesis of recursive digital filters," IEEE Trans. Audio. Electroacoust., vol. 21, no. 6, pp. 500-505 , Dec. 1973.
    • S.-C. Pei and J.-J. Shyu, "Design of 1-D and 2-D IIR Eigenfilters," IEEE Trans. Signal Processing, vol. 42, no. 4, pp. 962-966, April 1994.

    • T. Lin and L. O. Chua, "On Chaos of Digital Filters in the Real World," IEEE Trans. Circuits Systems, vol. 38, no. 5, pp. 557-558 , May 1991.
